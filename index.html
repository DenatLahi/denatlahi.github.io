About
Denat Lahi

Hi, I'm a 15-year-old wildlife photographer and digital artist from Skåne, Sweden. I have been learning photo manipulation since I was 13 years old, this is when my interest for photo-creation began. I love capturing and processing photos. Today I mainly photograph wildlife.

What I really enjoy about wildlife photography is the patience and dedication required to capture that perfect photo. Every photo I capture is always going to be unique.
