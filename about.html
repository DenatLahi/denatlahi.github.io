About Me

About
Denat Lahi

Hi, I'm a 15-year-old wildlife photographer and digital artist from Skåne, Sweden. I love capturing and then processing my photos. I have been learning photo manipulation since I was 13 years old, which gives me an advantage when it comes to photography. I mainly focus on photographing wildlife.

What I enjoy about wildlife photography is the patience and dedication required to capture that perfect photo. Every photo I capture is always going to be unique.
